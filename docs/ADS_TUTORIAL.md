## Part 1: For Advertisers

### What is Philosify Ads?

Philosify is a platform where people analyze songs, books, films, and news through philosophical lenses. While users engage with the platform — waiting for their analysis, browsing debates, exploring the philosophy constellation, or reading news — your ad appears. These are highly engaged, intellectually curious users who spend time thinking deeply about ideas.

Your ad appears in two placements:
- **Sidebar** — Next to music, literature, news, debate, and constellation panels. Supports 5, 10, 15, or 20 second durations.
- **Constellation** — Inside the interactive philosophy history graph. 5 seconds.

Every impression is signed, verified, and tracked. You pay per 1,000 impressions (CPM). You control the budget, targeting, schedule, and creative.

---

### Step 1: Create Your Account

1. Go to **https://ads.philosify.org**
2. Click **Apply now**
3. Fill in:
   - **Company name** — Your brand or organization
   - **Email** — Your business email (this becomes your login)
   - **Password** — Minimum 8 characters
   - **Website** — Your company website
4. Click **Create account**

Your application is reviewed by an AI vetting system. If your score is 80 or above, you are approved instantly. If below 80, the Philosify team reviews your application manually (typically within 24 hours).

You will receive an email when your account is approved.

---

### Step 2: Explore Placements

Before creating a campaign, understand where your ad will appear:

1. From your dashboard, click **Placements** in the navigation
2. You will see two placement types:
   - **Sidebar Interstitial** — Appears alongside analysis results. Supports 5s, 10s, 15s, and 20s durations. CPM ranges from $6 to $12 depending on duration.
   - **Constellation Panel** — Appears inside the philosophy knowledge graph. 5 seconds only. $4 CPM.
3. Each placement shows a visual mockup of how your ad will look in context

---

### Step 3: Create a Campaign

1. Click **New Campaign** from the dashboard or navigation
2. Fill in the campaign form:

**Campaign Basics**
- **Campaign name** — A descriptive name for your reference (e.g., "Spring Book Launch")
- **Goal** — Choose between Balanced, Reach, or Engagement
- **Budget** — Your total budget in dollars (minimum $10)

**Placement Preferences**
- **Placement** — Balanced mix, Sidebar only, or Constellation only
- **Duration** — Best fit (let the AI decide), or choose a specific duration (5s, 10s, 15s, 20s)

**Audience Targeting** (optional)
Select who should see your ad. Leave empty to reach everyone. Available targeting:
- **Genres** — Music genres your audience listens to (Rock, Classical, Jazz, etc.)
- **Philosophies** — Philosophical schools they're interested in (Objectivism, Stoicism, Existentialism, etc.)
- **Languages** — User's interface language (English, Portuguese, Spanish, etc.)
- **Engagement** — Usage level (Casual, Regular, Power User)
- **Countries** — Geographic targeting by country
- **Content** — What features they use (Music Analysis, Books, Cinema, Colloquium, News, Quiz)

**Destination**
- **Destination URL** — Where users go when they click your ad

**Schedule** (optional)
- **Start date** and **End date** — Run your campaign in a specific window
- Leave empty for "as soon as possible" delivery

**Creative**
- **Upload your own** — Provide your ad image (PNG, JPG, GIF, or WebP, max 2MB)
- **Philosify creates it** — Write a creative brief describing what you want. The Philosify team will design it for you (creative fee applies).

3. Click **Generate plan**

The AI budget planner analyzes your inputs and generates an optimal allocation:
- How many impressions per placement
- Estimated clicks and reach
- Cost breakdown per placement and duration
- Recommended schedule

4. Review the plan summary. If satisfied, click **Create campaign**.

---

### Step 4: Upload Your Creative

If you chose "Upload your own":

1. Your campaign is created in **draft** status
2. Upload your ad image (the file picker appears during campaign creation)
3. Supported formats: PNG, JPG, GIF, WebP
4. Maximum file size: 2MB
5. Recommended dimensions: 300x250 for sidebar, 250x120 for constellation

If you chose "Philosify creates it":

1. Your campaign enters the **creative production** workflow
2. The Philosify team receives your brief and designs an ad
3. You receive an email when the draft is ready for review
4. Go to your campaign detail page to see the draft
5. Click **Approve draft** to accept, or **Request revision** to ask for changes
6. The cycle repeats until you approve

---

### Step 5: Pay and Launch

1. Go to your campaign detail page (click the campaign name in the Campaigns list)
2. You will see a **Pay** button with the total cost
3. Click **Pay** — you are redirected to Stripe Checkout
4. Complete the payment with credit card
5. After payment:
   - If you uploaded your own creative: your campaign goes **live immediately**
   - If Philosify created the creative: your campaign goes live after creative approval

You receive an email confirming payment, and another when the campaign starts delivering.

---

### Step 6: Monitor Performance

**Dashboard** — Your overview shows:
- Available balance
- Total campaign investment
- Campaigns awaiting payment or creative review
- Recent campaign status

**Analytics** — Click **Analytics** in the navigation:
- **Time-series chart** — Daily impressions over the last 7, 14, or 30 days
- **Overview stats** — Total impressions, clicks, CTR, spend for the period
- **Per-campaign breakdown** — Delivery percentage, impressions, clicks, CTR, and spend per campaign
- **CSV export** — Download your data for external reporting

**Campaign Detail** — Click any campaign to see:
- Delivery progress (percentage and absolute numbers)
- Order status per placement
- Creative preview

---

### Step 7: Manage Your Campaigns

From the campaign detail page, you can:

- **Pause** — Temporarily stop delivery. Your undelivered impressions are preserved.
- **Resume** — Restart delivery after a pause.
- **Cancel** — Stop the campaign permanently. Undelivered impressions are refunded proportionally via Stripe.
- **Edit URL** — Change the destination URL for draft or pending campaigns.

---

### Step 8: Billing

**Add Funds** — If you want to pre-fund your account:
1. Go to **Billing** in the navigation
2. Choose a preset amount ($50, $100, $250, $500, $1000) or enter a custom amount
3. Click **Add funds** — redirects to Stripe Checkout
4. Funds are added to your balance immediately after payment

**Transaction History** — View all deposits, campaign spends, and refunds with dates and descriptions.

### Step 9: Account Settings

Go to **Settings** to:
- Update your company name, website, and contact email
- Change your password
- View your account approval status
- Delete your account (requires typing "DELETE" to confirm)

### Frequently Asked Questions

**How are impressions counted?**
An impression is counted when your ad is at least 50% visible in the user's viewport for 1.2 seconds or more. Each impression is signed with a cryptographic token to prevent fraud.

**Can I target specific users?**
Yes. You can target by music genre, philosophical interest, language, engagement level, country, and content preferences. Targeting is optional — leave it empty to reach all users.

**How often will the same user see my ad?**
Each user sees your ad a maximum of 3 times per day (frequency cap). This prevents ad fatigue.

**Can I change my ad after it's live?**
You can change the destination URL for campaigns that haven't started delivering. For active campaigns, you can pause, resume, or cancel.

**What happens if I cancel?**
Undelivered impressions are refunded proportionally. The refund goes back to your Stripe payment method.

**Do premium users see ads?**
No. Premium users are excluded from ad delivery.

**What file formats are supported?**
PNG, JPG, GIF, and WebP. Maximum 2MB.

## Part 2: For Agencies

### What is the Agency Program?

Agencies can manage ad campaigns on behalf of multiple clients from a single dashboard. You earn a commission on every impression your clients' ads receive.

---

### Step 1: Register Your Agency

1. Go to **https://ads.philosify.org/agency/signup**

   (You can also reach this from the landing page by clicking **Agencies** in the top navigation)

2. Fill in:
   - **Agency name** — Your agency's company name
   - **Email** — Your business email
   - **Password** — Minimum 8 characters
   - **Website** — Your agency website
   - **About your agency** — Brief description of your agency and the clients you serve

3. Click **Create Agency Account**

---

### Step 2: Add Clients

1. From your agency dashboard, click **Clients** in the navigation
2. Click **Add Client**
3. Enter the client's email and company name
4. The client receives an advertiser account linked to your agency

Each client's ad spend generates commission for your agency.

---

### Step 3: Manage Client Campaigns

1. In the Clients list, click **Campaigns** next to a client
2. You see all of that client's campaigns with status, impressions, and budget
3. From here you can create, monitor, and manage campaigns on their behalf

---

### Step 4: Set Commission Rates

1. In the Clients list, each client has an editable commission rate field
2. The default is 10% (set by the platform)
3. You can adjust between 0% and 50% per client
4. Commission is calculated automatically on every impression and credited to your agency balance in real time

---

### Step 5: Track Earnings

1. Click **Earnings** in the navigation
2. You see:
   - **Available balance** — Cash ready for payout
   - **Total earned** — Lifetime commissions
   - **Total paid out** — Lifetime payouts
   - **Commission rate** — Your default rate
3. **Transaction history** — Every commission credit and payout with dates and descriptions

---

### Step 6: Request a Payout

1. When your balance reaches **$100** or more, the **Request Payout** button becomes active
2. Click **Request Payout**
3. Your balance is deducted and the request is submitted
4. The Philosify team processes payouts within 3-5 business days
5. You receive an email confirming the request

---

### Agency Dashboard Overview

Your dashboard shows at a glance:
- **Balance** — Available funds
- **Total Earned** — Lifetime commissions
- **Clients** — Number of active clients
- **Commission Rate** — Your default rate
- **Client list** — With status, commission rate, and quick access to campaigns
- **Payout eligibility** — Shown when balance exceeds $100

---

### How Commission Works

```
User sees client's ad
  -> Impression recorded
  -> Client charged (CPM rate)
  -> Your commission calculated (% of CPM)
  -> Commission credited to your balance instantly
```

Example: Client's ad has a $10 CPM. Your commission rate is 10%. For every 1,000 impressions, you earn $1.00.

---

### Agency FAQ

**Can I create campaigns for my clients?**
Yes. You have full access to create, manage, pause, resume, and cancel campaigns on behalf of any client linked to your agency.

**When can I request a payout?**
When your balance is $100 or more.

**How long do payouts take?**
3-5 business days after the request is submitted.

**Can I change a client's commission rate?**
Yes. Edit the rate directly in the Clients list. Changes apply to future impressions only.

**What if a client cancels a campaign?**
The client receives a proportional refund. Your earned commission on delivered impressions is not affected.

**Can I have multiple agency accounts?**
Each email address can be associated with one agency account. Contact us if you need a different arrangement.
